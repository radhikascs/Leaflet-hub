VIEW-hub

Vaccine Information & Epidemiology Window

VIEW-hub is an interactive platform designed for stakeholders at all levels and domains to visualize real-time data on vaccine introduction and impact, to help inform decision making related to the:

  •   Acceleration of vaccine introduction
  •   Optimization of vaccine program implementation

Introduction to VIEW-Hub

Visualizing progress in global vaccine introduction

VIEW-hub will help stakeholders…

  •   Monitor global & country-level progress
  •   Identify gaps in vaccine introduction, implementation, evaluation
  •   Identify strategies to reduce those gaps
